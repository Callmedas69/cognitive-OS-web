# PRODUCT.md — cognitiveOS website

**Register:** brand / marketing (the design is the product).

**What it is:** Marketing + docs site for cognitiveOS, an AI-powered ICM (Interpreted
Context Methodology) filesystem scaffold for developers with ADHD. Promise: never lose
context between sessions; open your laptop and know exactly what to do.

**Audience:** solo developers with ADHD, comfortable with CLI/markdown/npm; vertical focus
blockchain/Web3. Secondary: devs without ADHD wanting better context management.

**Single conversion goal:** run `npx cognitiveos init` or open the GitHub repo. No pricing,
signup, or email capture.

**Concept:** a calm, horizontal-scroll deck of full-viewport sections — 7 product sections
(Overview, The Problem, How It Works, Six Zones, Commands, Open Source, Start) + a footer panel
that scrolls in horizontally too. Top nav and bottom timeline stay fixed while sections pan
under them. Editorial big-type (Bebas) with a per-section mood-color morph. Calm is the product
promise (ADHD audience): no overstimulation, no dark mode, no audio.

**Source of truth for facts:** the codebase `cognitiveos@0.3.0` and the reconciled docs in
`00_THE-VAULT/04_PROJECTS/01_development/cognitiveos-website/docs/`. Never claim post-MVP
features (Hermes, Mission Control TUI, Obsidian sync, etc.) as shipped.
